# Privacy Policy – Bible Marathon

**Last updated:** January 2026

Bible Marathon (“Bible Marathon”, “we”, or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how user data is collected, used, and protected when using the Bible Marathon mobile application (“the App”), in accordance with **Google Play User Data**, **Families Policy**, and **COPPA** requirements.

---

## 1. App Audience

Bible Marathon is a Bible reading application intended for **general audiences**, including **children under the age of 13**.  
We take special care to handle children’s data responsibly and in compliance with applicable child privacy laws.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

The App collects **limited personal data** necessary to provide its core functionality.

### Personal Data Collected
- **Name (or display name)**
- **Email address**
- **Profile picture**

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- Precise location data
- Phone numbers
- Contacts
- Messages or chat data
- Device identifiers for advertising
- Sensitive personal information

---

## 3. How We Use Data

Collected data is used **only** for the following purposes:
- Account creation and authentication
- Displaying user profile information within the App
- Account recovery and support
- Improving app functionality and reliability

**User data is not used for advertising or marketing purposes.**

---

## 4. Children’s Data and Families Policy Compliance

Bible Marathon complies with **Google Play Families Policy**:

- We collect **only essential data**
- We do **not** display personalized or behavior-based ads
- We do **not** sell or share children’s personal data
- Data is used solely to support the App’s functionality

### Parental Control
Parents or legal guardians may:
- Review the personal data collected from their child
- Request deletion or correction of their child’s data

Requests can be made using the contact information below.

---

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **not sell or share user data** with third parties.

Data may be shared only:
- With service providers necessary to operate the App (e.g., secure authentication), under strict data protection obligations
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect the rights and safety of users

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect user data, including:
- Secure storage and transmission
- Limited access to personal data
- Use of trusted third-party services that comply with Google Play policies

Despite these measures, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

---

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

Personal data is retained **only as long as necessary** to provide App services or meet legal requirements.

Users or parents may request account and data deletion at any time.

---

## 8. User Rights

Users (or parents/guardians for children under 13) may:
- Request access to personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of personal data

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements.  
Updates will be posted within the App and on the Google Play listing.
